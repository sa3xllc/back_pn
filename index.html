Protocol Logic

This is a daily spine rehabilitation protocol built around Stuart McGill's flexion-intolerant framework. Every session opens with a pain gate. Score of 7+ restricts you to Blocks 1, 2, and 5 only (spine reset, Big 3, breathing). Below 7 you get the full protocol including glute activation, nerve glides, and stability work.

When To Progress Back

When your walking pain drops below 4/10 consistently for 7+ consecutive days and session pain is trending down, we layer strength work back in one session at a time alongside this protocol.

Cues That Matter

Curl-up: one hand under lumbar arch, lock ribcage, don't flatten back. Side plank: feet stacked, straight line ear to ankle. Bird dog: lock spine first, then extend. Clamshells: pelvis stacked and still, movement is small. Stir the pot: only if pain is 3/10 or below that day.

Red Flags — See a Professional

Pain radiating below the knee. Numbness or tingling in legs or feet. Loss of bladder or bowel control. Pain that wakes you from sleep. No improvement after 2 weeks of consistent protocol use.
